Left-Handed Mobile UX Study

This study looks at how mobile interfaces can be made more comfortable for left-handed users. You'll try 8 short interactive mockups (4 pairs, each with a standard version and a left-hand-adjusted version) and answer a few quick questions after each one.

Takes about 10–15 minutes. Best experienced on a smartphone. Your responses are anonymous and used only for academic research purposes as part of a master's thesis.
